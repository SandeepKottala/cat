How to Boil an Egg

  1. Put eggs in a pot filled with cold water.
  2. Bring the wayer to a boil.
  3. Take the pot of the heat,cool it,and let it sit for 12 minutes.
  4. Remove the eggs from the hot water and cool them by running water over them or placing in a bol of ice water to cool off.
  5. Peel and eat.
